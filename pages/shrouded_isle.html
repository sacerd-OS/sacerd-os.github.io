[06/??/2024] the shrouded isle

The Shrouded Isle is an indie game published by Kitfox Games about ritual sacrifice the banality of evil. I've been obsessed with it for a long time and I inevitably ran out of gameplay and fan content to keep me going: I even discovered a significant portion of the developer content, such as the game's original Ludum Dare submission and storyboards for the ending cinematics. Naturally, I decided to do what any person of my skillset would: decomp and reverse, to see what I can find.
